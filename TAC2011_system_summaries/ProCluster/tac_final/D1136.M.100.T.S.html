The Yangtze River has experienced the worst drought in more than a century. China's central and western regions are suffering from a severe water shortage. The Three Gorges dam has opened its floodgates to feed the Yangtze River. A long drought in the upper reaches of the Yangtze saw the water level in the middle reaches decline. Global warming will negatively impact China's ecological, social and economic systems, especially farming, animal husbandry and water supply. Over 9,000 chemical plants line the banks of China's longest waterway The world's largest hydropower project has been releasing more water since late December. In the latter half of this century production of wheat corn and rice in China will drop by as much as 37 percent
