Water levels of both the Ganges and the Brahmaputra have increased. Heavy monsoon rains and snowmelt have inundated Bangladesh. Hundreds of thousands of people fled their homes in India. In 2004 floods killed more than 700 people and 38 percent of the country More than 200,000 people have been marooned in northern Bangladesh The floodwaters affected more than 4.5 million people in 36 districts The flood situation worsened in the country's northern and northeastern districts At least 10 people have died of drowning and snake-bites in the area. Landslides triggered by heavy rains killed at least 126 people At least 38 people have been killed in flood-related incidents
