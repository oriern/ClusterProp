A fire turned a dance hall jammed with teenage revelers into a deathtrap. A fire killing 60 people and injuring about 180. Hundreds of teen-agers jammed into an upstairs hall The fast-spreading fire completely gutted the building. The fire was the deadliest in modern Swedish history. Police said most victims choked to death on smoke and poisonous gases The dance was attended mostly by immigrants or children of immigrants. At least 57 were in intensive care with serious injuries.
