The Justice Department and 20 states are suing Microsoft. The United States vs. Microsoft Corp.is playing out against a century of antitrust laws Both sides are citing the same rulings to support their opposing arguments. The Supreme Court ruled in favor of bringing legal order to 100 years of clashing antitrust doctrines. The case focuses on Microsoft's Windows XP operating system. Microsoft has invoked the Sherman Act to file a civil suit that seeks to change the company's business practices, not a criminal suit The government says its objective is to curb illegal monopolistic business practices. Microsoft asserts that the case is intended to give the government control over Microsoft.
